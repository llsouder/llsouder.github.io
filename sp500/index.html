What This Page Is For

S&P 500
Large-cap U.S. market benchmark

The S&P 500 tracks 500 of the largest publicly traded U.S. companies and is one of the main ways people judge the overall stock market.

This page gives you a clean place to check the index and view a live chart.

Common ticker symbols:
• SPX = S&P 500 index
• SPY = popular ETF that tracks the S&P 500
• VOO = Vanguard S&P 500 ETF
